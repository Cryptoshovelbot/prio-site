WHAT WE BUILD

Data, automation, and intelligence for real estate

We don't sell a fixed product. We build bespoke tools powered by our real-time scanning of Idealista, Fotocasa, and Badi.

Market data and reports

Automated reports on pricing, volumes, trends, and days on market. By neighborhood, city, or custom zone. Delivered daily, weekly, or on demand in the format you choose.

Competitive monitoring

Track what your competitors publish, at what price, in which zones. Detect price changes, listing removals, and market movements before they become trends.

Automated prospecting

Detect every new listing the moment it appears. Automated lead extraction, contact information, and scoring. Pige at scale across the entire Spanish market.

Custom integrations

Real-time API feeds, webhook notifications, CRM sync, Google Sheets export, Notion integration, custom dashboards. Whatever fits your workflow, we plug into it.

CONCRETE EXAMPLES

Solutions we can build for you

Not hypothetical features. Real solutions we've built or can build within days.

PROSPECTING

A system that alerts you the moment a new listing appears in your zone, with the owner's phone number, before any other agent sees it. You call first, you get the mandate.

COMPETITIVE INTEL

A weekly report showing every price change in your market. Which competitors dropped their price, by how much, and when. Spot opportunities and adjust your strategy.

MARKET ANALYSIS

A live dashboard of rental prices by neighborhood in Barcelona, with average price per m², median time on market, and volume trends. Updated every hour, exportable to Excel.

AUTOMATION

An automatic daily export of all new sale listings under €300k in Valencia, sent to your CRM with photos, surface, location, and contact. Zero manual work.

MONITORING

Track when listings in your portfolio zone get removed or republished. Know instantly when a competitor's listing disappears or reappears at a different price.

CUSTOM API

A REST API that returns all active listings in a given zone, with filters on price, surface, rooms, publication date. Paginated, authenticated, documented. Plug it into anything.

FAQ

Common questions

How much does it cost?

Depends on the scope. A custom alert system might start at a few hundred euros per month. A full market intelligence platform costs more. We always give you a clear quote before building anything.

Which platforms do you scan?

Idealista, Fotocasa, and Badi across all of Spain. We scan continuously, in real time, and detect new listings within minutes of publication.

How fast can you deliver?

1 to 2 weeks for most solutions. We're not building from scratch. We're adapting our existing scanning infrastructure to your specific needs.

Can I get raw data via API?

Yes. REST API, webhooks, or any format you need. Authenticated, rate-limited, documented. We build the endpoints that match your use case.

Is this different from your B2C product?

Completely. The B2C product is a Telegram bot for individuals searching for an apartment. What we build for professionals has nothing to do with it. Custom data, custom delivery, custom scope.

Can you expand to other countries?

Our architecture supports multiple platforms and countries. We're focused on Spain today, but expanding coverage to a new market is feasible. Let's discuss your needs.
