1. Service Description

Prio is a property alert service. We monitor publicly available listing data from real estate platforms (including but not limited to Idealista, Fotocasa, Badi, and others) and send notifications to users when new listings match their defined search criteria.

2. Detection Speed and Performance

Prio detects new property listings ahead of the native push notifications sent by the platforms we monitor. This speed advantage is structural and continuously measured, but it varies depending on conditions.

  • Performance objective, not contractual guarantee. The detection speed advantage is a performance objective, not a binding guarantee for any individual listing. Actual detection time varies depending on the listing type, time of day, third-party platform load, and network conditions.

  • Republications (listings re-promoted by the owner or agent): the detection mechanism works differently for these. Prio makes its best effort to detect and deliver them as quickly as possible, but the speed advantage over the platforms is not guaranteed for this type of listing.
