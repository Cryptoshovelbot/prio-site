The Problem

You're not too slow. The platforms are.

You set up alerts on Idealista, Fotocasa, or Badi. But here's what the platforms don't tell you: they don't send a notification for every listing. And when they do, there's a delay. By the time you get the push, other people have already seen the listing and called. That's why you keep missing places, even when you react instantly.

And if you're searching across platforms, you're checking three separate apps all day, still missing listings that only appear on one of them.

Prio monitors all three platforms and notifies you for every single new listing, before the platforms send their own alerts. Everything arrives in one Telegram chat, with the exact location and a ready-to-send WhatsApp message to contact the owner. You always arrive first.
