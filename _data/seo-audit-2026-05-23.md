# SEO Audit — getprio.io — 2026-05-23

## Health Score: 6.5 / 10

## Executive Summary

The site has a **solid technical foundation** (canonicals, OG tags, JSON-LD on most pages, clean sitemap) but is held back by **very low organic traffic** (12 clicks / 1,267 impressions in 28 days) and several content/configuration issues that limit ranking potential. The most impactful finding is that **llms.txt is severely inaccurate**, claiming coverage of 15 platforms and 13+ countries when the site only covers Spain with 3 platforms. Two pages have oversized meta tags, and 13 city pages under `/es/` have a `lang="en"` attribute mismatch.

Clicks rose from 8 to 12 (+50%) period-over-period, but impressions dropped from 1,880 to 1,267 (-33%), suggesting possible ranking fluctuations or Google re-indexation.

## 6. Plan d'Action -- Top 3 Priorities

### Priority 1: Fix llms.txt (URGENT -- 30 min)

**Why:** Every LLM citing Prio right now is getting wrong pricing, wrong platform list, wrong country coverage. This actively misleads potential customers.

**Action:**
- File: `/opt/prio-site/llms.txt`
- Remove all non-Spain platforms (SeLoger, Bien'ici, Immobiliare, Rightmove, etc.)
- Remove all countries except Spain
- Fix pricing: Roommate EUR 4.95/month, Renter EUR 19.90/month, Buyer EUR 49.90/month
- Update alerts count to 130,000+
- Remove B2B pricing if not publicly offered
- Add "expanding to other European markets in 2026" as future, not current

### Priority 2: Fix meta tag issues + add blog images (2-3 hours)

**Why:** Titles >60 chars and meta descriptions >160 chars are truncated in SERPs. Zero images in blog articles hurts engagement and image search traffic.

**Actions:**
1. `/opt/prio-site/pro/index.html` line 6: Shorten title to <= 60 chars (e.g., "Prio for Professionals -- Real Estate Tech in Spain")
2. `/opt/prio-site/pro/index.html` line 7: Shorten meta desc to <= 160 chars
3. `/opt/prio-site/blog/documents-rent-apartment-spain/index.html`: Shorten title to <= 60 chars (e.g., "Documents to Rent in Spain (2026) -- Complete Checklist")
4. Add at least 1 relevant image per blog article (neighborhood photos, document checklists, platform screenshots)
5. Update robots.txt: replace "Claude-Web" with "ClaudeBot", add "Google-Extended" with Allow

### Priority 3: CTR optimization on high-impression pages (1-2 hours)

**Why:** Several pages have good positions (5-12) but 0 clicks. Better titles can convert impressions to traffic.

**Actions:**
1. `/blog/idealista-scams-how-to-spot-fake-listings/` -- 198 impressions, 0.5% CTR, pos 9.2. Current title "Idealista Scams 2026: 37% of Listings Are Fake" is good but could use power words. Consider: "Idealista Scams 2026: Spot Fake Listings (37% Are Ghost)"

3. `/blog/barcelona-rental-market-2026/` -- 99 impressions, 0% CTR, pos 7.8. Title is fine but SERP snippet may not be compelling enough -- review meta description for hooks.
4. `/blog/how-to-find-apartment-barcelona-2026/` -- 86 impressions, 0% CTR, pos 11.5. Page ranks for "best websites to find apartments" (30 imp). Adjust title: "Best Websites to Find Apartments in Barcelona (2026 Guide)"
