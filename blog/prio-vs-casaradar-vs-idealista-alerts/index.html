Prio vs CasaRadar vs Idealista Alerts — Which Is Fastest in Spain?

June 20268 min read

If you're hunting for an apartment in Spain, you already know the problem: the best listings are gone within the hour. The question isn't whether to use alerts — it's which alert service actually gets you there first. This guide compares the three options people weigh up most often: Prio, CasaRadar, and Idealista's own built-in alerts.

We build Prio, so we have a stake here — but the comparison below is built on how these services actually work, not marketing claims, and we're upfront about where each one wins.

What Is the Fastest Apartment Alert Service in Spain?

Prio is the fastest, because of where it detects listings. Most alert tools wait for a listing to appear in a platform's public search results, then notify you. Prio detects new listings at the source — the moment they enter Idealista, Fotocasa or Badi — and sends a Telegram alert within seconds. That's typically minutes ahead of the platform's own push notification, which by our measurements arrives 3 to 10 minutes after publication.

In a market where the first three callers get the viewing and everyone else gets a waiting list, that head start is the entire game.

Prio vs CasaRadar: Breadth vs Speed

CasaRadar and Prio are the two services people compare most, because both go beyond a single platform. The honest difference comes down to breadth versus speed.

CasaRadar's strength is breadth. It aggregates listings from a wide range of housing portals into a single feed — useful if you want to cast the widest possible net, including smaller or niche sites.

Prio's strength is speed and depth on the platforms that matter most. Rather than covering every portal, Prio focuses on the three that carry the overwhelming majority of Spanish listings — Idealista, Fotocasa and Badi — and optimises for getting you there before everyone else. Every Prio alert also arrives ready to act on: photo, price, the landlord's phone number, the exact location, and a pre-written WhatsApp message so you can make contact in a single tap instead of typing while ten other people are already calling.

The one-tap difference

Speed only matters if you can act on it. A Prio alert lands on Telegram with everything you need to contact the landlord immediately — no opening the app, no copy-pasting, no writing a message from scratch. By the time a native alert even reaches the people relying on it, you've already sent yours.

Are Idealista's Own Alerts Fast Enough?

For a relaxed search in a quiet town, yes. For Barcelona, Madrid, Valencia or any competitive market, no. Idealista's (and Fotocasa's) native push notifications arrive 3 to 10 minutes after a listing is published. In that window, a desirable flat can already collect dozens of enquiries. Native alerts also only cover their own platform — if you rely solely on Idealista, you systematically miss the ~40% of Fotocasa listings that never appear there, plus the entire Badi room market.

Which Apartment Alert Service Should You Choose?

  • Choose Prio if your priority is being the first to call on Idealista, Fotocasa and Badi, with contact details ready in every alert.
  • Choose an aggregator like CasaRadar if you want maximum coverage across many smaller portals and speed is a secondary concern.
  • Rely on native Idealista alerts only for low-competition searches where a few minutes' delay won't cost you the apartment.
