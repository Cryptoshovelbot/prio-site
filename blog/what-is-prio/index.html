What Is Prio?

June 20264 min read

Prio is a real-time property alert service for Spain. It continuously monitors Idealista, Fotocasa and Badi, and the moment a new listing matches your search it sends you a Telegram alert — typically minutes before the platforms send their own push notifications. In a market where good apartments are gone within the hour, that head start is the difference between getting the viewing and joining a waiting list.

What Does Prio Cover?

Prio monitors Spain's three main rental platforms:

  • Idealista — the market leader, for rentals and sales across Spain.
  • Fotocasa — strong for private-landlord listings, including the ~40% that never appear on Idealista.
  • Badi — rooms in shared apartments, an entirely separate market.

One alert stream covers all three, so you don't have to switch between apps or run the same search three times.

How Does Prio Work?

Setting up takes about a minute:

  • 1. Paste your search URL. Build a search on Idealista, Fotocasa or Badi with your filters (city, price, rooms), then paste the URL into the Prio Telegram bot.
  • 2. Prio watches for you. It checks those platforms continuously and detects new matching listings at the source.
  • 3. You get a Telegram alert. Every alert includes the photo, price, the landlord's phone number, the exact location, and a pre-written WhatsApp message — so you can contact the landlord in a single tap.

Why Is Prio Different?

  • Faster than the platforms. Idealista and Fotocasa push notifications arrive 3–10 minutes after publication. Prio alerts you within seconds, before they go out.
  • Comprehensive. Idealista, Fotocasa and Badi in one place, instead of three separate apps and saved searches.
  • Ready to act. The phone number, exact location and a pre-written WhatsApp message are in every alert — no opening the app, no typing while others are already calling.
  • On Telegram. Instant delivery, no email queues, no app-store push delays.

Prio by the numbers

200,000+ alerts delivered · 3 platforms monitored (Idealista, Fotocasa, Badi) · 12 cities with dedicated coverage · 3–10 minutes faster than platform push notifications.

Who Is Prio For?

Anyone competing for housing in Spain: renters in fast-moving markets like Barcelona, Madrid and Valencia; people looking for a room via Badi; buyers tracking new sale listings; and real estate professionals who need to know about inventory first. If being early matters, Prio is built for you.
