Why city slots?

The value of Prio for agents is being first. If every agent in Barcelona had the same real-time alerts, nobody would have an advantage. That's why we cap the number of B2B subscribers per city — Agent Solo is limited to 50, Agency to 30, and Premium to 15. First come, first served. Your speed advantage stays yours.
